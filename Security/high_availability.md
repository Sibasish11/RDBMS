# High Availability (HA) in RDBMS

High Availability (HA) refers to the ability of a database system to remain **operational and accessible with minimal downtime**, even in the presence of failures such as hardware crashes, network issues, or software errors.

---

## Why High Availability is Important

- Prevents business downtime
- Ensures continuous access to critical data
- Improves user trust and system reliability
- Essential for banking, e-commerce, healthcare, SaaS systems
