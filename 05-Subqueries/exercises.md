## JOIN Exercises

1. Display customer name, order id, product name, quantity, and total price.
2. Find total revenue generated by each customer.
3. Find total sales per product.
4. Display customers who never placed any orders.
5. Find top 3 customers based on total spending.
6. Display monthly revenue.
7. Find customers who ordered more than 5 items in total.
8. Display highest value order.

## Subquery Exercises

9. Find employees earning more than company average salary.
10. Find products priced above category average.
11. Find customers who placed more orders than average customer.
12. Find second highest salary.
13. Find customers who never placed an order using subquery.
14. Find products that are most expensive in each category.
15. Find orders higher than customer's previous maximum order.


## Correlated Subquery Exercises

18. Find products costing more than their category average.
19. Find customers whose order value exceeds their own average.

## Window Function Exercises
